About me

Growing up in a family of down-to-earth craftsmen and women, I learned from an early age to approach projects in a pragmatic but detailed oriented way.

My vivid imagination and passion for creating things from scratch led me to pursue a career in Design with now almost 10 years of work experience in Retail, Visual Merchandising, Prop Making and Set Design where working in various settings and different dynamics throughout the years helped me immensely to enhance my team-work and communication skills.

Always eager to grow and advance my skills even further, I recently decided to train as a Full Stack Web Developer, hoping that my drive to solve problems and optimise things, in addition to my creative background, will come in handy and help me open yet another chapter in my life.
